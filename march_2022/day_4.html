Your goal is to unlock all the locks as quickly as possible. Type "wait [number]" to wait that number of minutes, "use [key name] key" to use that key, "log" to show all your commands so far, "undo" to undo, or "restart" to restart. You can type multiple commands in at once with semicolons separating them.
