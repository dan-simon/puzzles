Rules and Links

To solve "legitimately", don't look at the answer checker or anything with solutions in its filename or in any subdirectory titled solutions. Looking at HTML/CSS/JS files or at data from the GitHub repository is allowed but not required or even that helpful except for puzzles that clearly says otherwise, as long as you avoid the files mentioned in the first sentence. Each set of puzzles linked below is independent of the others. The answer checker takes a second (it may be slower or faster depending on your computer) make it slightly harder to use its code to get the answer by brute force.

Unfortunately, the puzzles aren't testsolved.
