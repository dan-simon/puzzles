The words given are the meanings of Greek roots or in some cases Latin roots. These roots can be combined to make words of the given lengths. There's some ambiguity about what the actual roots are and what combines with what, which could be somewhat of an obstruction for solvers. Multiple meanings are often given to hopefully help a bit with this ambiguity.
